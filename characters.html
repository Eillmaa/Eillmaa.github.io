The Elements

There are 7 elements in Genshin Impact. All playable characters can use one of the elements. These unique powers come from the Archons themselves, who bestow their gifts and elemental powers on other beings. Elements are crucial to success in Genshin Impact - learning how to use them could be what saves you, young traveler.

ANEMO

The wind element of Genshin Impact. Very versatile and can spread other elements except Geo. It is considered a neutral element. Used for Swirl and Elemental Absorption reactions. One of the characters that uses the Anemo element is Xiao!

HYDRO

The water element in Genshin Impact. Useful for putting out fires. Weakest against Cryo and Electro. Used for Vaporize, Electro-Charged and Frozen reactions. Nuevillette is one of the characters that uses the power of the Hydro element!

PYRO

The fire element in Genshin Impact. Great for burning wooden and Cryo shields. Weakest against the Hydro element. Used for Melt, Overloaded, and Vaporize reactions. One of the characters that uses the Pyro element is Arlecchino!

DENDRO

The plant element in Genshin Impact that was released with Sumeru. Dendro can trigger several reactions and chain reactions depending on the elements. It cannot interact with the Anemo and Geo element. Alhaitham is one of the Characters that can use this element!

GEO

The rock element of Genshin Impact. Usefull for destroying rock structures and rock shields. Weakest against other Geo damage and the Overloaded reaction. Interracting with the other elements except Anemo will trigger the Crystalized effect. One of the characters in the game that can use this element is Zhongli!

ELECTRO

The lightning element in Genshin Impact. Weakest against Cryo and Pyro. Used for Overloaded, Superconduct, and Electro-Charged reactions. Raiden is one of the characters that can harness the power of electro!

CRYO

The ice element in Genshin Impact. Can slow and freeze characters. Weakest against the Pyro Element. Used for Melt, Superconduct, and Frozen reactions. One of the characters that can use this element is Eula!
